LitRPG Critic is now live.

We're an independent editorial site covering the best books in LitRPG, progression fantasy, dungeon core, and GameLit fiction. Our goal is simple: help readers find their next great read in a genre that publishes hundreds of titles every month.

## What We Cover

- **Ranked lists** — curated, ranked by community ratings and editorial judgment
- **Series reviews** — deep-dives into the books worth your time
- **New releases** — regular coverage of what's just dropped
- **Sub-genre guides** — whether you want dungeon core, system apocalypse, or cultivation fiction

## Our Data

Book data comes from [LitRPGTools.com](https://litrpgtools.com), the largest community database for the genre. We surface that data editorially — every list reflects genuine curation, not just a sort filter.
